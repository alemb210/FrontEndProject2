What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to the human condition on a non-physical level. This means our emotional, social, and psychological wellbeing. It is often an overlooked aspect of a person's health due to the non-immediate danger. However, our mental health affects many aspects of our lives, such as our decision-making, general mood, relationships with people, and reaction to stressors.

How does this affect Tech students?

Tech students are often under heavy stress for multiple reasons. Tech, as a specialized high school, is particularly rigorous in it's education. The culture places a heavy emphasis on grades and scores, amping up stress and deteriorating the self-worth of many students who do not perform to their liking. Additionally, the high-school ages can also place a significant amount of stress on students. Teenagers often feel pressure to conform with their peers, and thus can often become stressed over other people's perception of them.
