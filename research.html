As shown in the image to the left, the whole world struggles with mental health problems, depression as an example. While many factors influence the data shown in the graph, the variance could be attributed to the different lifestyles in these countries as well as the lack of mental health awareness.


Share of the population with depression, 2016 Source: ourworldindata.org

Another similar but broader study achieved the same results as above. This gives a further scope on the reach of mental illness, as the scale is significantly larger. When other mental health issues and substance are included, the scale more than triples. America, in particular, reported a ~25% share of the population in this study.
