What are some symptoms of poor mental health?

Symptoms of poor mental health include:

  • Constant feelings of sadness
  • Extreme feelings of stress (fear, worry, guilt)
  • Lack of enjoyment or enthusiasm in previously-enjoyed activities
  • Extreme mood changes
  • Brain fog (inability to concentrate)

Conversely, what does good mental health look like?

"Good" mental health is often characterized by:

  • Being confident in yourself
  • Having an optimistic outlook on life
  • Remaining calm and collected, even under pressure
  • Being in control of thoughts and feelings
  • Finding ease and enjoyment in communicating with others
